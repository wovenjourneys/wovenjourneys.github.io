Culturally Immersive Trips For The Restless Souls!

Nepal

With the "travel like a local" approach, Woven Journey’s goes beyond tourism and aims to connect humans on a deeper level, through cultural immersion. If you crave a deeper connection and are looking for a unique opportunity to travel to a destination off the beaten path, grab your suitcase and get ready to explore the colorful Nepali culture, learn about the heritage, meet the living goddess, stay with the friendly locals and eat authentic and delicious food. All you’ll have to do is show up because the rest is already taken care of!

Photography

Interested in displaying the human connection through her travels and explorations of the human form,

About

Being born and raised in Nepal, Sophiya created Woven Journeys to showcase the majestic beauty of her country by contributing to its people in a meaningful way. By teaming up with the locals, she has created jobs for the young generation of Nepal who reside in the country, connecting them with the world beyond theirs, in hopes that they can live their dreams through their own means.
